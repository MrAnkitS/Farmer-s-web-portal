Get Rs 80,000 Govt Scholarship to Pursue Career in Agriculture & Veterinary Sciences, Apply Before 31st March

Govt. of India is inviting applications for Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE) under the INSPIRE scheme. INSPIRE Scholarship for Higher Education 2021-22 is an initiative for students who have passed class 12. The selected students will receive scholarships up to Rs 80,000 per annum for the duration of the course. The Last Date to apply for this scholarship & avail its benefits is 31st January 2022.

The scheme is focused on attracting meritorious students to study basic and natural sciences at college & university level and to pursue research careers in both basic & applied science areas including engineering, medicine, agriculture & veterinary sciences. This is one of the best scholarship opportunities for all Indian students!

INSPIRE Scholarship for Higher Education: Eligibility

  • Age: 17-22 years


  • Have passed the Class 12 examination from any central/state board in India with aggregate marks within the top 1% of the class.


  • Pursuing a B.Sc/B.S./Int.M.Sc./M.S. degree course in Basic and Natural Sciences from any recognised institute/college/university in India.


  • Among the top 1000 rank holders in JEE of IIT or AIPMT/NEET and pursuing a B.Sc./B.S./Int.M.Sc./M.S. course in Basic and Natural sciences.


  • Pursuing an integrated MS course from NISER, IISERs or be a scholar of KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana), Department of Atomic Energy Centre for Basic Sciences, NTSE (National Talent Search Examination), JBNSTS (Jagadish Bose National Science Talent Search) or be a medalist of International Olympiad pursuing bachelor/master level course in Natural Science.
